Mississauga Youth Action Committee.

MYAC strives to represent the 145,000 youth in the city of Mississauga and works to voice their opinions and better engage youth in their community. MYAC promotes the well-being of youth (aged 12-24) in the city of Mississauga. MYAC advocates on their behalf to various community stakeholders in order to inspire positive change and uphold a youth-friendly city.
